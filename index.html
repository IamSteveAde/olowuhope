About Us

We are building a Nigeria where no one is left behind, starting from Owu

The Olowu Hope Initiative is a community development movement rooted in royal leadership and civic compassion.

Founded by His Royal Majesty, Prof. Oba Salaudeen Matemilola, it is built on a simple belief: when people are fed, empowered, healed, and educated, nations thrive.

Why We Started

Because Everyone Deserves a Chance to Rise

In too many communities, dreams are held back by poverty, poor health, conflict, and a lack of access. The Olowu Hope Initiative was born to restore dignity — by empowering women, feeding children, providing healthcare, and building peace. Led by His Royal Majesty, Prof. Oba Salaudeen Matemilola, we’re starting from home, but our mission is national.
